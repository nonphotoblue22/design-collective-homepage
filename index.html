Santa Monica College
Design Collective

The Santa Monica College Design Collective (SMCDC) is a student-run organization at Santa Monica College. We aim to build a thriving design community among the students interested in design at Santa Monica College. We create a space to share, learn, and practice design for all students interested in the field of design.

What We Do

Inviting Design Experts to Campus

We invite design experts from the local businesses and startups to come talk at Santa Monica College. See a list of past speakers.

Design Sprints & Awesome Workshops

SMCDC creates and runs workshops on making students better designers and better people. We've hosted portfolio development workshops, resume building workshops, design sprints, and much more.

Making Awesome Newsletters

Our newsletters are awesome. Get tapped into what's going on around the design at SMC. We spread the word about local design events, upcoming SMCDC events and updates, students looking to collaborate on design competitions, and so much more.

Multidisciplinary Collaboration

There was a vacuum of design collaboration at SMC. The SMCDC aims to change that. We're open to all design desciplines at Santa Monica College, and we try to have as wide of a range of representation during our meetings. We see diversity as a profound advantage.
